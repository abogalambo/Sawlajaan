Sawlajaan صَوْلَجَانٌ

Rules :

  • Letters: A, b, t, th, j, 7, 5, d, z', r, z, s, sh, S, D, T, Z', 3, g, f, q, k, l, m, n, h, w, y
  • Tashkeel: letter + a , letter + e/i, letter + o/u
  • Taa marboota: t'
  • Tanween: uN, aN, iN/eN
  • madd: aa, ee, oo
  • Alef with hamza: A, E, O or U
  • Alef without hamza: a at the start of a word
  • Alef maqsoora: Y
  • Hamza's: 2, _2, @/@2
  • Shadda: letter + *
  • waw aljamaa3a: w-a / oo-a
  • bel: be-al
  • combined letters when ignoring tashkeel e.g. seen-haa: s-h
